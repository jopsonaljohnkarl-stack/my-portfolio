Why a Specialist

Why not just hire
a developer?

Building automations is a different discipline — tools, logic, and operations. Here's the practical difference.

Specialist vs Developer
Delivered in days, not months

Developers build from scratch. I connect existing tools using APIs, webhooks, and no-code platforms — faster deployment, lower cost, and no custom codebase to maintain.

Tailored system vs Off-the-shelf tool
Built around your exact process

SaaS tools solve general problems. I map your specific workflows — your tools, your data, your edge cases — so the automation fits your operation, not the other way around.

Clear handoff vs Black box
Your team can own and maintain it

Every system I build is documented before handoff. Your team will know exactly what runs, why, and how to adjust it — no dependency on me to keep the lights on.

Process

How I Work

A clear, proven process from first call to a working automation in 2–4 weeks.

01
Understand the Bottleneck

Schedule a 30-minute intro call. No prep needed — just come with your biggest operational pain point.

02
Map the Workflow

We map the tools involved, the data that needs to move, and the approvals or handoffs that happen along the way.

03
Plan the Build

I define the workflow logic, choose the right tools, and shape the solution around how your team already works.

04
Build & Test

I build against real use cases, check for bad data and duplicate records, and work through the edge cases that usually break automations later.

05
Launch & Handover

Once the workflow is stable, I document it, launch it, and make sure your team can understand and maintain it confidently.
